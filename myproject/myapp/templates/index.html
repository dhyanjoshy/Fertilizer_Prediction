About

Fertilizer Prediction is a technology driven solution for crop and agriculture.

The system process data from various source to provide farmers with actionable insights and predictions. These predictions help farmers to make informed prediction what fertilizer can be used. The ultimate goal of the system is to increase efficiency, productivity and probability in agriculture while reducing waste and environmental impact.
